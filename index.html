Who we are

An (unofficial) local chapter of the DSA! We're a group of residents of San Mateo, Menlo Park, Palo Alto, Mountain View, Los Altos, Sunnyvale, and other neighboring towns. By coming together, we can bolster and create initiatives that make life easier and more equitable for our slice of the Bay Area and beyond.

To get a sense of how the DSA operates as a national organization, and how SVDSA might relate to that national organization, check out the Spring Platform. It provides a good sense of our strategic and tactical goals: how to handle growth from new members in a "vibrant, democratic" fashion, and how to ensure "transparent and accountable structures of leadership and decision-making."

Meeting up

We're slowly organizing a set of standing meetings, but historically the SVDSA has met in Mountain View one of two ways:

  • Socializing at the St. Stephen's Green pub: meeting, mingling, hearing what's new and where everyone's at
  • Organizing at our General Meetings, typically held at the Mountain View Community Center. Here we get deeper into plans for the near, medium, and long-term.

We're currently cementing a permanent routine for these SVDSA specific events. In the meantime, check out our calendar below, and follow our Facebook and Twitter to stay up to date on what we're up to.

Get in touch with us at SVDemSoc@gmail.com. We'd love to hear from you!
